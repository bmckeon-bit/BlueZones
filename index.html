Proposed Terms

Letter of Intent.

Six principles to anchor the first 24 months — sweat equity, global exclusive, and a chemistry window to prove the pipeline.

01

Global Exclusive

FOC as exclusive wellness RE development partner for Blue Zones branded communities, worldwide.

02

24-Month Chemistry Window

Sweat equity from both sides. Either a predictable pipeline emerges, or we part as friends.

03

Right of First Look

FOC vets all Blue Zones inbound real estate leads. Qualified deals go to joint pipeline.

Brand Stewardship

Blue Zones retains final approval on all projects. FOC enforces annual compliance audits. Pull the license if fidelity drops.

Shared Economics

Combined licensing fees, development fees, and promotes. Fees fund day-to-day. Promotes align the upside. Specific split TBD.

Identify 2–3 pilot projects. Leverage active Blue Zones cities for immediate deal flow.
